Condense milk Pudding

Description

Condensed milk pudding, also known as caramel pudding or creme caramel, is a delectable dessert enjoyed worldwide for its silky-smooth texture and rich sweetness. This classic dessert typically consists of a layer of caramelized sugar at the base, topped with a velvety custard mixture made from eggs, milk (full-fat for the best flavor), condensed milk, vanilla extract, and sometimes flour. The custard can be baked or steamed to perfection, creating a delightful sweet treat with a perfect balance of sweetness and creaminess. Condensed milk plays a key role in enhancing the richness and sweetness of the pudding, making it a favorite for those with a sweet tooth. This dessert is often served chilled and can be garnished with caramel sauce, powdered sugar, vanilla ice cream, or whipped cream for an extra touch of indulgence. Enjoyed by many, condensed milk pudding is a timeless and comforting dessert that brings joy and nostalgia with every spoonful.

Ingredients

  • Condense Milk 400ml
  • Milk 400ml
  • Vanilla extract
  • Four eggs

Syrup

  • A cup of sugar 200g

Directions

  1. In a blender, put all the ingredients and blend for 2~3 minutes until smooth.

  2. With a pan over medium heat, add a cup of sugar and stir until it is completely caramelized, then add 30ml of hot water and continue stirring.

  3. After mixing everything, turn off the heat and pour the syrup into the pudding mold and then add the dough, gently and slowly, as the dough cannot mix with the syrup, it is necessary to do the process in that order, so that at the time After un-molding, the syrup stays on top of the pudding.

  4. Then, cover the pan with aluminum foil and place the pan in a water bath in a preheated oven at 180Â°C for 40 minutes. Note: The time will depend on the power of your oven, I recommend that you add hot water when placing it in the water bath speed up the process.
