Condense milk Pudding!

Ingredients

  • Condense Milk 400ml
  • Milk 400ml
  • Vanilla extract
  • Four eggs

Syrup

  • A cup of sugar 200g

Directions

STEPS

  1. In a blender, put all the ingredients and blend for 2~3 minutes until smooth.

  2. With a pan over medium heat, add a cup of sugar and stir until it is completely caramelized, then add 30ml of hot water and continue stirring.

  3. After mixing everything, turn off the heat and pour the syrup into the pudding mold and then add the dough, gently and slowly, as the dough cannot mix with the syrup, it is necessary to do the process in that order, so that at the time After un-molding, the syrup stays on top of the pudding.

  4. Then, cover the pan with aluminum foil and place the pan in a water bath in a preheated oven at 180Â°C for 40 minutes. Note: The time will depend on the power of your oven, I recommend that you add hot water when placing it in the water bath speed up the process.
